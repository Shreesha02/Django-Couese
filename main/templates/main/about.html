HILLTOWN RESORT

Be One With Nature



Welcome to HillTown Resort

Nestled on the serene banks of Lake Vembanad, amidst the vast stretch of tranquil, emerald green backwaters, HillTown Resort stands as the epitome of luxury and heritage. Located in the heart of Kerala, one of India’s most beloved holiday destinations, our resort promises an unparalleled experience of opulence and tradition.

Our Heritage

Spanning 25 acres of lush greenery, HillTown Resort exudes the timeless charm of Kerala’s rich heritage. Our resort has been honored with the prestigious World Travel Award as India’s Leading Resort for four consecutive years, a testament to our commitment to excellence.

Accommodations

At HillTown Resort, we offer a variety of accommodations to suit your desires:

  • Luxury-laden Rooms: Elegantly designed with modern amenities.
  • Villas and Suites: Spacious and meticulously crafted for ultimate comfort and privacy.

Wellness & Recreation

Our array of facilities ensures a rejuvenating and enjoyable stay:

  • Ayurmana, the Ayurveda Spa: Indulge in traditional Ayurvedic treatments and therapies.
  • Swimming Pools: Multiple pools to relax and unwind.
  • Health Club: State-of-the-art fitness equipment to keep you active.

Dining

Experience a culinary journey with our diverse dining options:

  • Multi-cuisine Restaurants: Offering global flavors to tantalize your taste buds.
  • Specialty Restaurants: Featuring exquisite dishes crafted by our expert chefs.
  • Traditional Kerala Teashop: Savor the local flavors and culture.

Attractions

Discover countless attractions both within and around the resort, from cultural experiences to natural wonders, all offering the simple luxuries of life within the grandeur of royalty.

Our Promise

HillTown Resort is not just a place to stay; it’s a destination that provides a unique blend of luxury, heritage, and nature. Whether you seek relaxation, adventure, or a cultural immersion, our resort is the perfect retreat for creating unforgettable memories.

Come, experience the magic of HillTown Resort, where luxury meets heritage in the heart of Kerala.

Feel free to contact us for more information or to make a reservation. We look forward to welcoming you to HillTown Resort, your home away from home.
